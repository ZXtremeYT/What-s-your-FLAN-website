What's your FLAN?

What's your FLAN? has a variety of food called "leche flan" in which you can taste a good, sweet, tongue melting flavor. What's your FLAN? consists of 2 main layers: the base and the topping.The base contains the leche flan itself while the toppings gives more flavor to the base.The topping can have a variety of fruits, vegetables, and other flavors you can try!.
